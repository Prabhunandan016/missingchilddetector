Welcome to Missing Child Portal

This platform helps families and authorities report, track, and find missing children across the country. Explore the portal to report a missing child, view missing children data, and stay informed about child safety measures.

How This Portal is Useful

The Missing Child Portal is designed to connect families, communities, and authorities in the search and reporting of missing children. By using the portal, users can take immediate action, stay informed, and contribute to child safety initiatives.

Report Missing Children

Submit detailed information about missing children to alert authorities and communities immediately.

View Missing Children Data

Access a comprehensive database of reported missing children to stay informed and help locate them.

Stay Updated on Safety Tips

Learn preventive measures, safety tips, and guidelines to protect children from potential dangers.

Connect With Authorities

Coordinate with local authorities and organizations for faster action in locating missing children.

Ways to Use the Portal

The portal is simple and user-friendly. You can report missing children, check recent cases, and follow up on ongoing investigations. Authorities and NGOs can also use it to coordinate recovery efforts effectively.

Step 1: Report a Child

Fill out the online form with all the required details about the missing child.

Step 2: Review Reports

Check the portal for newly reported missing children in your area.

Step 3: Support Search Efforts

Share reports and information with local communities to aid in locating children.

Step 4: Stay Informed

Access safety guidelines and preventive tips to avoid similar situations in the future.
